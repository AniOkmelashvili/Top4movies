12 Angry Men

Release Date:March 25, 1957

Director:Sidney Lumet

Starring: Henry Fonda, Martin Balsam, John Fiedler, Lee J. Cobb, E.G. Marshall

Genre:Drama, Detective

Synopsos:The defense and the prosecution have rested and the jury is filing into the jury room to decide if a young Spanish-American is guilty or innocent of murdering his father. What begins as an open and shut case soon becomes a mini-drama of each of the jurors' prejudices and preconceptions about the trial, the accused, and each other.

Awards

  • 16 WINS & 12 NOMINATIONS

Box Office:Grossed over $7.5 million worldwide
